Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we launch.

What exactly is Omnexus and who is it for?

Omnexus is a unified creative platform designed to streamline the entire 3D workflow. Instead of jumping between different apps for modeling, animating, and creating mockups, you can do it all in one place. It's built for modern creators: 3D artists, product designers, marketers, and AR/VR developers who want to move faster and bring their ideas to life without being slowed down by disconnected, legacy tools.

How is Omnexus different from other web-based 3D tools?

Other web-based tools are great for creating 3D visuals. But Omnexus isn’t just about making things look good — it’s about making them work. Instead of juggling multiple fragmented tools, Omnexus brings everything together in one place with powerful AI integration:

  1. Create and refine 3D objects — from simple shapes to polished, production-ready assets.
  2. Build interactive, functional experiences — our visual node-based editor makes adding logic, animations, and even AR elements as easy as drag and drop.
  3. Turn ideas into sales-ready content — instantly generate mockups, animate them, and craft marketing visuals that actually convert.

With Omnexus, you don’t stop at design. You go from idea → to interactive prototype → to real, engaging experiences — all without writing a single line of code.

What can I do with my creations? What formats do you support?

Anything you want! Omnexus is built to be the central hub of your creative pipeline. All assets are AR/VR-ready out-of-the-box. You can export your models and animations to standard industry formats like GLB, USDZ, FBX, and MP4, making them easy to use in game engines like Unity and Unreal, on websites, in social media marketing campaigns, or in other 3D software.

Do I need a powerful computer or any special software?

No. Omnexus is a fully browser-based platform. This means there are no heavy downloads or installations, and you don't need a high-end graphics card to get started. As long as you have a modern web browser and an internet connection, you can access the full power of Omnexus from anywhere.

How will your pricing work? What’s the philosophy behind the tiers?

While our final pricing is still being perfected, our philosophy is to make creativity accessible. We plan to have a range of tiers, including a Free plan perfect for exploring the platform. Our paid tiers (Pro, Ultimate) will be designed for professionals and teams who need more advanced features and higher usage limits, while our Enterprise plan will cater to large organizations. Our goal is to provide massive value at every level.

You'll be the first to receive an invitation when we open up more spots, and you'll also be eligible for exclusive launch-day offers. We'll be sending out invitations in batches as we scale up.
